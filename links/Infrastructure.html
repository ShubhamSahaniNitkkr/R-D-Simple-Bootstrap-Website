NITKKR offers the state of the art infrastructure to its students and scholars for research purposes. The Ph. D cell covers under its ambit inviting applications, short listing and giving admission to candidates as research scholars under the departments of Information Technology, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Management, Cyber Laws and Information Security, Bio-Informatics and Applied Sciences. The Institute has engaged students under a wide range of heads and disciplines within the Information and related technology domain ranging from pure IT fields such as -Soft Computing, artificial intelligence, Graphics animation and multimedia, Data Mining, Wireless and Sensory Networks, Service Oriented Architectures to Electronics and Communication Engineering topics such as VLSI Fiber Optics, Photonics, Robotics, Computer vision and Embedded systems. The Bio-Informatics Department under its academic concern has research interest in areas like Computational Biology, Structural Biology and Drug Designing, Modeling and Simulations of complex biological structures.
